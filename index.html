About

I'm a Front End Developer, based in Wakefield, West Yorkshire. I graduated from the University of Huddersfield in 2012 with a BSc (Hons) in Web Technologies. I currently have over 8 years’ experience in the web industry and my passion lies in building creative, user-friendly websites and email marketing campaigns. I’ve worked with some very well-known brands and have produced websites, landing pages, HTML5 Banner ads and emails for clients such as Asda, WorldPay, JML, Hermes, Halfords, Greggs, Virgin Active, Pets at Home, Iams, Vitality and nPower. My strengths lie in email marketing and I’m proud of the fact that I can build emails that display great across all of the major email platforms and devices.

I'm hard-working and passionate about what I do and I always put in maximum effort to ensure that the work that I produce is the best it can be. I’m not afraid of a challenge and like stuck in with a project. I believe that I’m easy-going and good to get along with.

In my spare time, I like to spend time with my Husband, our 2 children and our Border Terrier, Holly. I like to draw, create outlined illustrations listen to a mixture of music, watch Horror movies, and finally, I love to bake!
